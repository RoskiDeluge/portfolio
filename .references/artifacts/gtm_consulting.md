# Business Analytics & Web Tracking Audit 

## Roadmap for Fixing/Improving Tracking

• **Kickoff Calls (two 30 minute calls) to learn more abouth the goals of the organization/business.** 

• **GA4 & GTM Audit**  
  - Review GA4 data streams, events, and conversions for business alignment.  
  - Check GTM tags, triggers, and data layers for quality and consistency.  
  - Identify any gaps or overlaps that might impact data accuracy.

• **Tracking Roadmap**  
  - Provide a prioritized list of fixes and improvements.  
  - Deliver a clear plan with steps and timelines to enhance event tracking and reporting.  
  - Include best practices for ongoing adjustments and performance measurement.

• **Deliverables**  
  - A brief audit report outlining current strengths and improvement areas.  
  - A step-by-step roadmap detailing technical fixes, tracking enhancements, and required resources.
  - follow-up call to walk through the findings.

## Scope of Work

1. **KPI Analysis**  
   - Interview key stakeholders to define priorities and identify critical success metrics based on business objectives (e.g., lead generation, increased donations, or online sales).

2. **Audit**  
   - Evaluate your GA4 and GTM configurations to ensure tracking aligns with business goals, such as accurately monitoring online campaigns and identifying key conversion points.

3. **Deliver Recommendations**  
   - Provide prioritized, actionable solutions that include:  
     A. Enhancements for campaign tracking and conversion attribution to better understand which sales/donations channels are most profitable and deserve increased investment.  
     B. Upgrades for revenue tracking and sales attribution to precisely measure sales impact and refine your marketing strategies.

4. **Next Steps**  
   - Discuss any remaining issues surfaced during the assessment and possible handoff of tasks to internal teams or consultants.
